# Business Workflow

## Purpose

The Government Subsidy & Grant Disbursement Tracking System automates the complete lifecycle of subsidy management, from beneficiary registration to fund disbursement and monitoring.

---

# Workflow Overview

The business process consists of the following stages:

1. Beneficiary Registration
2. Login
3. Scheme Selection
4. Application Submission
5. Document Upload
6. Eligibility Evaluation
7. Field Verification
8. District Approval
9. Finance Approval
10. Fund Disbursement
11. Compliance Monitoring
12. Application Closure

---

# Detailed Workflow

## Step 1: Beneficiary Registration

A beneficiary registers in the system by providing personal details and creating login credentials.

---

## Step 2: Login

The beneficiary logs into the portal securely.

---

## Step 3: Browse Government Schemes

The beneficiary views available subsidy and grant schemes.

---

## Step 4: Submit Application

The beneficiary selects a scheme and submits an application.

---

## Step 5: Upload Documents

Required documents are uploaded for verification.

Examples:

- Aadhaar Card
- Income Certificate
- Land Ownership Certificate
- Bank Passbook
- Passport Photograph

---

## Step 6: Eligibility Evaluation

The system evaluates the application based on predefined eligibility criteria.

---

## Step 7: Field Verification

The Field Officer verifies the submitted information and supporting documents.

---

## Step 8: District Approval

The District Officer reviews the verification report and approves or rejects the application.

---

## Step 9: Finance Approval

The Finance Officer approves fund allocation.

---

## Step 10: Fund Disbursement

The subsidy amount is transferred to the beneficiary's registered bank account.

---

## Step 11: Compliance Monitoring

The system monitors project milestones and fund utilization.

---

## Step 12: Application Closure

Once all milestones are completed, the application is closed.

---

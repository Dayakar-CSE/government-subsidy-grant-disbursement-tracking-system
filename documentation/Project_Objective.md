# Project Objective

## Government Subsidy & Grant Disbursement Tracking System

### Objective

The objective of this project is to design and develop a centralized web-based application that automates the complete lifecycle of government subsidy and grant disbursement.

The system enables beneficiaries to apply for government schemes, allows officers to verify applications through multiple approval levels, manages fund disbursement, tracks compliance milestones, and provides reporting and analytics.

The application aims to improve transparency, reduce manual processing, minimize delays, and ensure efficient management of government welfare schemes.
